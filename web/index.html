        Rules of Palace
        1. The goal of Palace is to get rid of all your cards by beating the current state of the pile.

        2. If you have no playable cards, you must pick up the entire pile and end your turn.

        3. At the end of your turn, draw from the deck until you have 3 cards. If there are no more cards in the deck, no more cards are drawn.

        4. You start with 3 cards facing down and 3 cards facing up. These are your tower cards and they are not playable until there are no cards in your hand. Face down cards can not be played until the card above it is removed.

        Special Cards

        7. 
        W
        Wild. This card can be placed on anything. When played, the state of the pile is cleared and the player's turn is restarted.

        8. 
        B
        Bomb. This card can be placed on anything. When played, all cards in the pile including this card are discarded and the player's turn is ended.

        9. 
        R
        Reverse. This card can be placed on anything. When played the current game direction is reversed and the player's turn is ended.

        10. 
        HL
        Higher/Lower. This card can be placed on anything. When played, the player sets the current pile state to be higher or lower than the current state of the pile and the player's turn is ended. The next player must beat the current pile state by playing a card with a value equal to or Higher/Lower depending on what the previous player chose.

        11. 
        D
        Discard. This card can be placed on anything. When played, all cards in the player's hand are discarded, that amount is drawn from the deck, and the player's turn is restarted. If there is no cards remaining in the deck, this card becomes a regular value card 0

        12. 
        T
        Topswap. This card can only be played if the player has at least one more playable card. When played, the player chooses 2 tower cards to swap, and the player's turn restarts. The player must play another card after playing this card.

        13. 
        H
        Handswap. This card can only be played if there is at least one other player with a hand. When played, the player chooses 1 card from another player's hand and swaps hands with the owner of that card. The player's turn then restarts. The player must play another card after playing this card.
